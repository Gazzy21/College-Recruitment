Welcome to TCNJ

Here at TCNJ we are on a mission to help students learn, grow, and prepare for life after school.

About TCNJ

The College of New Jersey (TCNJ) is a respected public institution in Ewing, New Jersey, known for its strong undergraduate programs and commitment to academic excellence. With a vibrant campus community and dedicated faculty, TCNJ fosters student engagement and personal growth, preparing graduates to make meaningful contributions to their fields and communities.

Academics at TCNJ

The College of New Jersey (TCNJ) emphasizes rigorous undergraduate education across diverse disciplines. With dedicated faculty and a focus on critical thinking and experiential learning, TCNJ fosters collaboration and innovation. This commitment to academic excellence is reflected in high graduation rates and the success of its graduates.

Athletics at TCNJ

The College of New Jersey (TCNJ) features a strong athletics program in NCAA Division III, offering a variety of sports that promote teamwork and school spirit. Known as the Lions, TCNJ teams have a tradition of success, achieving high rankings and championships while emphasizing the balance between academics and athletics for student-athletes.

Campus Life at TCNJ

The College of New Jersey (TCNJ) offers a vibrant campus life that fosters community and engagement. With a range of student organizations, cultural events, and leadership opportunities, students can connect with peers and explore their interests. The picturesque campus provides spaces for socializing, studying, and recreation, while residence halls encourage a sense of belonging. TCNJ’s diverse activities and supportive environment help create a well-rounded college experience.
